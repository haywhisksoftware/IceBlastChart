In the game Defense of the Ancients 2 (DotA 2 for short), there is a hero named Ancient Apparition.
Ancient Apparition's Ice Blast ability can instantly shatter heroes with low hit points. Use these tables to figure out if your target will live, based on their maxium hit points.
Note that some abilities can prevent or delay death by frostbite. See Gamepedia for more information.
